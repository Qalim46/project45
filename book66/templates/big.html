Description

When the very existence of Universe is threatened by Big Bang, Earth needs a warrior, whose powers are out of this world. Siddharth Kumar Yadav aka Sky is a college geek studying Quantum physics and is unaware that he himself is the God particle, the dark aliens are looking for.

Story of Sky becoming the ultimate warrior Blu, the rider of flying Bike Pushpak. A mythical warrior, created by the ancient technology of Gods. A Legend from Ramayan, the rider of Pushpak and the wielder of Vajra, takes his true Blu Avatar. Can he fight and save the world from Big Bang? An epic science fiction saga of a SuperHero. An origin story of Big Bang Blu.

This is a Science-Fiction novel by a first-time Author and It has taken almost 5 years of research in Mythology, Science, Biology and Anti-Gravity. Author hopes that you readers like this blend of Mythology and Science and enjoy this attempt.
